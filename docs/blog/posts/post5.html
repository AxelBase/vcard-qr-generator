Static Sites + Dynamic QR Codes

Building Fully Static, Zero-JS-Framework QR Tools with SvelteKit

Published: November 20, 2025

This vCard QR generator runs entirely in your browser — no server, no analytics, no cookies. Here’s how SvelteKit makes that possible.

The Core Trick: adapter-static + Client-Side Generation

Using @sveltejs/adapter-static, the entire site builds to pure HTML/CSS/JS. All vCard and QR generation happens 100% client-side using vcard-creator and Qrious.

Key Technical Advantages

  • No backend = no attack surface
  • No database = no data collection
  • No cookies = no tracking
  • Works offline (after first load)
  • Deployable to GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, or any static host

Bundle Size Reality

Final production build: ~185 KB gzipped
QRious: 26 KB
vcard-creator: 8 KB
Everything else: vanilla SvelteKit

Privacy by Design

No telemetry. No Google Analytics. No Facebook Pixel. No “contact form” that phones home. Your contact data never leaves your device — not even temporarily.

Why This Matters

Most online QR generators log your data. Some inject tracking URLs. Some add their owny logo. This tool does none of that.

True privacy isn’t a feature — it’s the default when you remove the server.
